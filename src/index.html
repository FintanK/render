A Node.js and Grunt boilerplate that's all about speed and performance

Automate the task of ensuring your best practice web app is optimized for delivery to mobile, tablet and desktop. Reduce loading times and keep your users engaged.

Customize for an optimized user experience.

Bower

Install your application dependencies automatically.

Grunt

Automate your builds! Merge and minify your files. Generate sprites, cache manifests and webp images for your deployments.

Bootstrap and Sass

Autoprefixing to make your style cross browser.

Responsive Images

Different image sizes for different devices. All generated automatically and used via the picture element.

Caching

Cache project assets with a manifest file that's generated as your project progresses.

Compression

Make your assets even smaller before they are sent to the user.

Automated Testing

Build your application and run automated tests to verify it's functionality and ensure it meets your requirements.

Pagespeed

Run your builds against Google pagespeed automatically and get feedback on your bottlenecks.
